Protocol - Experiment 1

Step 1
Completed?
Dilute cells so they will be diluted to cell/well or cells/well with volume of µl/well.
Step 2
Completed?
Plate in well plate so that there are wells per dilution. Can plate up to clones/plate.
Step 3
Completed?
Culture remaining cells in TC flask ( mL/flask).
Step 4
Completed?
When TC flask is near-confluent, collect supernatant in tubes with mL each and store at °C.
Step 5
Completed?
Collect cells in mL centrifuge tube and spin down cells ( rpm, mins) to discard remaining supernatant and wash with PBS once. Resuspend in mL PBS and transfer to mL centrifuge tube to collect cell pellet ( rpm, mins), aspirating remaining PBS well. Store at -°C.
